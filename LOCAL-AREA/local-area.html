WELCOME TO

Plettenberg Bay

Plettenberg Bay, known for its stunning beaches and vibrant wildlife, is a coastal paradise in South Africa. Whether you're here to relax or explore outdoor activities, you'll find everything you need for a memorable stay.

EXPLORE

Top Attractions

Robberg Nature Reserve

Experience breathtaking hikes along the peninsula with stunning ocean views and wildlife sightings.

Beautiful Beaches

Relax on pristine sandy beaches like Central Beach and Keurbooms Beach.

Tsitsikamma National Park

Adventure awaits with ziplining, kayaking, and canopy tours in the indigenous forest.

Whale & Dolphin Watching

Take a boat tour to see majestic whales and playful dolphins in their natural habitat.

Getting Here

Plettenberg Bay is easily accessible by car and has its own regional airport. Located along the N2 highway, it's approximately 600 km from Cape Town and 210 km from Port Elizabeth.
