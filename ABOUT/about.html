About Us

Your trusted partner for unforgettable holiday homes along the stunning Garden Route.

Our Story

In 2023, Calm Waters Plett was founded with the vision of providing exceptional holiday experiences in Plettenberg Bay. As demand grew, we added more properties to offer guests a variety of luxurious and cozy stays close to nature's beauty. We continue to provide well-maintained properties with unmatched comfort and service for every guest.

Meet the Team

Kecia Barry is the Founder & Host of Calm Waters Plett. Together with her co-host, Lionel Barry, and the support of Daniel Barry, who maintains the properties, they ensure every guest experiences a seamless stay in our luxury holiday homes.
