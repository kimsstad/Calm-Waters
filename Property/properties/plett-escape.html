Plett Escape Flat

A stylish, cosmopolitan first-floor apartment perfectly located in the heart of Plettenberg Bay’s vibrant center. Within walking distance of top restaurants, beautiful beaches, and thrilling activities—hiking, mountain biking, abseiling, and ocean adventures. Gym and Plett Country Club just around the corner. Whether you seek relaxation or excitement, planning your stay here is effortless.

Sunny, spacious, modern open-plan living area with air-conditioning (heating & cooling), high-speed internet, and Google Home connectivity. The main bedroom features a queen-size bed; the second bedroom offers two single beds. Both rooms have large built-in wardrobes. The bathroom has a walk-in shower and handheld bidet sprayer. The kitchen includes microwave, dishwasher, coffee machine (Nespresso), mini-fridge, cookware, and utensils—no oven or freezer.

Fitted throughout with one-way double-glazed, sound-proof mirror glass for privacy and insulation. Enjoy morning coffee or sundowners on the private balcony (no braaing permitted) with high table and bar stools. This upstairs unit is accessed via an external staircase; children must be supervised on balcony and stairs. Additional comforts: TV with Netflix & Disney+, board games, books, dedicated workspace, portable fan, hairdryer, iron, and fresh linens.


Please Note – No braaing allowed on balcony. Children under supervision. Minimum stay 2 nights. Ragers are allowed. Rates vary by season—contact us to book your stay!
