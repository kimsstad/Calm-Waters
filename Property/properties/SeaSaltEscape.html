SeaSalt Escape

Welcome to SeaSalt Escape — a bright, spacious 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom home located in the peaceful Poortjies area of Plettenberg Bay. Perfect for up to 5 guests, this recently renovated home combines relaxed comfort with modern coastal design, just a short stroll from the lagoon and nearby shopping centre.

The open-plan living space includes a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, washer, stove, fridge/freezer, and all essentials for a comfortable stay. The lounge features a Smart TV and WiFi for streaming and connectivity. Step outside to a lovely entertainment area with pool, braai, and comfortable outdoor seating — ideal for sunny afternoons.

The main bedroom is upstairs and en-suite with a private balcony, while the two downstairs bedrooms share a full bathroom with bath, shower, and toilet. Stylishly furnished and pet-friendly, SeaSalt Escape offers the perfect balance of tranquility and convenience, close to everything Plettenberg Bay has to offer.
