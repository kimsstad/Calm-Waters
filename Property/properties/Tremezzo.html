About this space

Welcome to Tremezzo, a spacious and elegant beachside home perfectly positioned on Central Beach in Plettenberg Bay. With direct beach access and sweeping ocean views, this refined retreat offers relaxed luxury just steps from the sand.

Designed for large family get-togethers or group stays, Tremezzo comfortably accommodates up to 10 guests across generous indoor and outdoor living spaces, blending comfort, privacy, and one of Plett’s most iconic coastal locations.

The space

This expansive 735 m² home features five stylish en-suite bedrooms, each fitted with either a king-size bed or twin singles. Thoughtfully designed across two levels, the home includes two fully equipped kitchens, two lounges, two dining rooms, and multiple entertainment areas.

Indoor fireplaces create a cozy atmosphere, while a dedicated games room with table tennis adds to the home’s family-friendly appeal. Modern conveniences include ceiling fans, Rinnai gas heaters, Wi-Fi, DSTV, inverter backup power, full laundry facilities, and a dedicated workspace.

The living areas flow seamlessly onto outdoor patios with a gas braai, perfect for relaxed meals while enjoying ocean views. Guests can swim in the pool, unwind on the patios, or step through a private gate for direct access to the beach. Swimming towels are provided for convenience.

Starter essentials including tea, coffee, milk, sugar, and toiletries are supplied on arrival to ensure a comfortable beginning to your stay.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Located within the secure and upmarket Whale Rock Heights estate, this home offers a peaceful setting with sweeping ocean and mountain views. The elevated position provides privacy and a calm atmosphere, while still being close to Plettenberg Bay’s main attractions.

Beautiful local beaches are just 3.3 km away, ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and coastal walks. The iconic Robberg Nature Reserve, known for its dramatic cliffs and hiking trails, is only 3.5 km from the property.

For everyday essentials, Robberg Bay Shopping Centre is conveniently located 2.4 km away and offers supermarkets, restaurants, and a range of useful services.

Whale Rock Heights combines security, natural beauty, and easy access to beaches, nature reserves, and town amenities, making it an excellent base for exploring Plettenberg Bay.

Getting around

A car is recommended for getting around Plettenberg Bay and the surrounding Garden Route. Secure parking is available on the property, including garages and driveway space for up to six vehicles.

From the estate, beaches, Robberg Nature Reserve, and shopping centres are all a short drive away. The estate itself is quiet and ideal for short walks, while driving offers the most convenient way to explore the area.

Taxi and chauffeur services can be arranged for trips into town, to nearby beaches, or local restaurants if preferred.
