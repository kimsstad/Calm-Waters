Tremezzo Villa

Located directly on Central Beach with private gate access, Tremezzo Villa is a luxurious 5-bedroom, 5.5-bathroom family-friendly haven with breathtaking ocean views. Enjoy morning walks on the sand, days by your private pool, and sunset braais on the patio.

Designed for large groups, Tremezzo features two fully equipped kitchens, spacious open-plan lounges and dining rooms, indoor and outdoor fireplaces, and a games room. All bedrooms are en suite, offering king or twin beds, ceiling fans, and thoughtful amenities like hairdryers, WiFi, DSTV, an inverter backup, and a dedicated workspace.

A private gate provides direct beach access while lush gardens, sun loungers, and outdoor dining spaces complete your seaside escape. With a starter kit of basics, full laundry facilities, and self check-in, this villa is the perfect coastal retreat in Plettenberg Bay.
