Sanctuary Studio Suite

Discover the charm of our Sanctuary Studio Suite—perfectly located near the beach, Robberg hike, and local shops. This private space features a cozy bedroom, en-suite bathroom, kitchenette, seating area, and a separate entrance on the upper floor of a welcoming home.

Relax on the shared patio with garden views, enjoy a dip in the expansive pool, or savor a sunset barbecue. The suite includes unique décor with original artwork, a fully equipped kitchenette (microwave, air fryer, Weber), and a private bathroom.

Take a short 900m walk or cycle (bikes provided) to a stunning 4 km beach or visit the nearby shopping center and café just 50m away.

Please Note Rates vary by season—contact us to book your stay!
The guesthouse is equipped with solar panels so the South African load-shedding will not impact your stay.
