Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

The area offers sweeping views across the Tsitsikamma Mountain Range, the Robberg Peninsula, and the idyllic surroundings of Plettenberg Bay. A 4 km stretch of beach, extending from Beacon Isle Hotel to Robberg, is conveniently just 900 m from the house.

The downtown valley, a mere 3 km away, hosts supermarkets and beachside and mountainside restaurants. Further along the road, the town offers a variety of fine eateries, art establishments, and diverse shops. Beyond Robberg Beach, Look Out Beach and Main Beach are located just below town, with a nearby lagoon providing crystal-clear waters and safe swimming for all ages.

A small entrance gate provides a convenient pathway for a 900 m walk or cycle (bicycles provided) to one of the world's most spectacular beaches, spanning over 4 kms, as well as an upmarket shopping center within 50 m with a chic brunch café offering breakfast, lunches, and cakes.

Getting around

While Plettenberg Bay offers taxi services, having your own car is the most convenient option. Parking is readily available throughout the area, with well-marked parking bays and sidewalk parking commonly used.
