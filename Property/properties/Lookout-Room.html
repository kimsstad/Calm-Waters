Lookout Studio Suite

This pet-friendly studio suite is conveniently located near the beach, Robberg hike, and shopping centers, with easy access to main roads for seamless travel. Enjoy a private bedroom, en-suite bathroom, kitchenette, seating area, parking, and a separate entrance on the upper floor of an inviting home. Unwind with morning coffee or a sunset barbecue on the private patio with garden views, overlooking the expansive swimming pool and captivating sunrises.

The suite is tastefully decorated with unique pieces, including original paintings by the host's mother, a renowned local artist. The kitchenette nook is perfect for preparing breakfasts, lunches, and light dinners. The cozy sitting area provides a welcoming space to relax with a book, play a game, or plan your day. Step outside to the shaded balcony, equipped with a barbecue and comfortable seating, offering panoramic views of Robberg and the nearby shopping area.

Guests have access to the front garden, pool, and a convenient outdoor shower. A small gate leads to a scenic 900m path to one of the world’s most beautiful beaches. Additionally, well-behaved pets are warmly welcomed, with the opportunity to interact with our own pets, including a bulldog named Honey and a Maine Coon cat named Nala. Experience the best of Plett’s pet-friendly amenities and beautiful beaches.


Please Note Pricing varies by season, and availability may change. We encourage guests to inquire about current rates and available dates to ensure the best experience during your stay.
