About this space

Welcome to Arrowood Flat, ideally located within walking distance of restaurants, shops, and the beach. This vibrant retreat offers stunning ocean views, a refreshing pool, and the security of a gated complex.

The property is situated next to a busy road — the lively sounds add character without sacrificing tranquility. With a full kitchen, secure parking, braai facilities, and DSTV, Arrowood Flat is ideal for a comfortable, hassle-free stay. NO MATRICULANTS OR “RAGERS” ALLOWED.

The space

Arrowood Flat is a self-catering unit within a secure, gated complex. It accommodates up to four guests across two bedrooms and two bathrooms.

Bedroom 1: Two single beds (can be arranged separately, en-suite bathroom with shower)
Bedroom 2: Two single beds (placed side by side, separate bathroom with shower)

The open-plan kitchen and lounge flow onto a garden and patio with beautiful ocean views. A short walk within the complex leads to the communal swimming pool, set in a lush garden, with access to a games area including table tennis and darts.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Arrowood Flat is situated within a residential complex, surrounded by neighboring homes and just across the road from a well-known local restaurant.

The apartment is a short 5-minute walk from the main street running through town, where guests can enjoy a wide variety of shops and an excellent selection of restaurants offering something for every taste. Additional recommendations can be found in the provided Guidebook.

With its central location and easy access to dining, shopping, and local attractions, the apartment offers a convenient and lively base for exploring Plettenberg Bay.

Getting around

On-site parking is available for one motor vehicle. Taxi services are also readily available for guests who prefer not to drive.
