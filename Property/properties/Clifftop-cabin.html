About this space

Nestled on the Plettenberg Bay cliffs, this coastal retreat offers a serene escape for couples or solo adventurers seeking a reconnection with nature. Our snug Clifftop Cabin is thoughtfully designed with a minimalist aesthetic.

Located in tranquil farmland just outside Plettenberg Bay, the property combines countryside tranquility with breathtaking coastal views. Guests can enjoy the wild ocean, scenic hiking trails, and the splendor of what Plett has to offer, all within a 10 km radius.

The space

The cabin features an open-plan layout that includes a bedroom with a queen bed, dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. The en-suite bathroom has a shower with stunning panoramic views.

Decorated in calming oceanic tones, the cabin is surrounded by untouched South African fynbos and expansive views of the Indian Ocean. Large doors open onto the deck, creating a peaceful space for relaxation and quiet reflection.

⚠️ Disclaimer: Guests stay at their own risk. The owners are not liable for any injury, accident, loss, or damage during the stay. Use of the pool, stairs, or facilities is at your own discretion.

Guest access

Guests have exclusive access to a private hiking trail directly from the property.

A pebble beach is located at the base of the hike, with hidden rock pools along the rugged coastline, accessible via a scenic 1 km walk.

Pristine white sand beaches and designated swimming spots are reachable by a short 6 km drive.

Other things to note

  • Beach access from the property leads to a rocky, pebbled beach and may not be suitable for swimming unless using nearby rock pools.
  • If booking for 4 guests, another cabin is available nearby.
  • South Africa may experience electricity load shedding. A schedule will be provided during your stay, and severe outages may affect Wi-Fi and related services.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

The cabin is set on serene farmland bordering Plettenberg Bay, perched atop dramatic cliffs overlooking the expansive Indian Ocean. The coastal atmosphere flows seamlessly into the space, offering sweeping views of waves crashing against the rugged shoreline where sea and sky meet.

Guests can relax on the covered patio and take in the awe-inspiring scenery, or explore a variety of hiking and walking trails ranging from gentle strolls to more invigorating routes, all surrounded by nature and open landscapes.

Getting around

A network of footpaths winds along the cliffs and farm boundaries, providing peaceful walks with stunning ocean views. A designated parking bay is located approximately 20 metres from the cabin.

A car is recommended for trips into town, which is located between 5 and 10 kilometres away. Plettenberg Bay offers grocery stores, shops, restaurants, coffee spots, wineries, and a wide range of outdoor and adventure activities in the surrounding area.
