Clifftop Luxury Cabin

Nestled on the cliffs of Plettenberg Bay, Clifftop Cabin offers tranquility and breathtaking coastal views, perfect for couples or solo adventurers. Located in quiet farmland, it's within a 10km radius of Plettenberg Bay's natural beauty, including beaches and hiking trails.

The cabin features an open-plan layout with a bedroom, dining area, and a fully-equipped kitchen. The en-suite bathroom offers panoramic views, and the deck provides a peaceful sanctuary with ocean views.
