About this space

Nestled on a hillside above Plettenberg Bay, this upmarket and luxurious apartment is the elegant sister unit to La-Lapa, offering breathtaking ocean views that create an unforgettable sense of escape. Elevated above the bustle of town yet close to modern conveniences, it provides a private and indulgent setting ideal for couples.

Designed with romance in mind, the apartment is perfect for honeymooners and couples seeking privacy, relaxation, and elevated coastal living in Plettenberg Bay.

The space

As you enter, you are welcomed into a sleek, contemporary open-plan layout. The modern kitchen is fitted with quality appliances and a bar counter, flowing into a spacious lounge with comfortable seating, a fireplace, and a smart TV, creating a warm and inviting space to unwind.

The open-plan bedroom is a private sanctuary with uninterrupted sea and ocean views, complemented by a cosy snug area ideal for reading, relaxing, or enjoying a quiet glass of wine. The beautifully designed bathroom features a freestanding bath and a centrally positioned rain shower for a truly indulgent experience.

Large openings lead onto a private verandah with sweeping coastal views. Guests have exclusive use of the pool and outdoor area, perfect for outdoor dining, relaxing in the sun, or soaking in the scenery of this romantic retreat for two.

Guest access

Guests have exclusive use of the entire apartment, including the private entrance, verandah, pool, and all indoor living spaces. Secure on-site parking is provided.

Other things to note

The apartment’s elevated hillside position offers expansive ocean views, privacy, and a strong sense of seclusion, while Plettenberg Bay’s shops, restaurants, beaches, and attractions remain conveniently close.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

La Med is perched on a hillside above Plettenberg Bay, offering sweeping ocean, bay, and sunset views from an elevated and tranquil setting. The surrounding natural landscape creates a sense of seclusion and calm, with elevated vantage points that minimise nearby activity.

While the setting feels peaceful and removed from the energy of town life, Plettenberg Bay’s main shopping areas, restaurants, and beaches are all close by, offering the perfect balance between relaxation and convenience.

The property is located close to a nearby settlement and sits above the N2 highway, meaning occasional traffic noise may be heard. The property remains secure and is managed to ensure guests’ comfort and peace of mind.

Getting around

Although the town is within walking distance, a car is recommended for most sightseeing and excursions in and around Plettenberg Bay, particularly when exploring beaches, nature reserves, and the wider Garden Route.

Taxi services and shuttle options are available in Plett, providing added convenience for guests who prefer not to drive during their stay.
