The space

Magnificent View is a fully renovated, second-floor apartment located in a well-established block just below Main Street, Plettenberg Bay’s vibrant hub of shops, restaurants, and grocery stores. The open-plan living area flows onto a patio with beautiful sea views, ideal for morning coffee or evening sundowners.

Only 1.1 km from the beach and directly across the road from Market Off Main, the apartment is perfectly positioned for guests wanting convenience, culture, and coastal living.

The apartment accommodates up to six guests across three bedrooms and two-and-a-half bathrooms.

Bedroom 1: King bed
Bedroom 2: Queen bed

The spacious lounge, dining area, and fully equipped kitchen provide everything needed for a comfortable self-catering stay. Neutral décor, quality finishes, and generous natural light create a calm, welcoming atmosphere throughout.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

The apartment block is ideally located just one road back from the vibrant heart of Plettenberg Bay. A short 300 m stroll brings you straight into town, where you’ll find a wide variety of shops, cafés, and restaurants catering to all tastes.

Directly below the apartment is Market Off Main, a popular local destination offering market-style shops, relaxed eateries, drinks, and beautiful sea views. It’s a lively, social space enjoyed by both locals and visitors, perfect for an easy afternoon or evening out.

Main Beach is only 1 km away and easily accessible, offering the classic Plettenberg Bay beach experience with golden sand and ocean views.

For additional tips and local recommendations, guests are welcome to consult the Guidebook for a detailed overview of the best spots in town and the surrounding area.

Getting around

Secure parking is available in the garage, with additional street parking nearby. Shops, restaurants, and the beach are within walking distance, although a car is recommended for exploring further afield. Taxi and shuttle services are also readily available in Plettenberg Bay.
