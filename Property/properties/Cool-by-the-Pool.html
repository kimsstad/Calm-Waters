Cool by the Pool @ Robberg

Nestled in the heart of suburban Plettenberg Bay and within walking distance of Sanctuary, Solar, and Robberg 5 beaches, Cool by the Pool @ Robberg offers a peaceful family escape. This spacious home sleeps up to 8 guests across 4 bedrooms and features a private pool, braai area, and lush garden surrounded by bird life.

Perfect for 2 families, the main house includes the main bedroom with en-suite, plus two children’s rooms sharing a full bathroom. The separate flat provides extra space for a second family, with a bedroom, sleeper couch in the lounge, kitchenette, and bathroom.

Guests can enjoy a fully equipped kitchen, comfortable living spaces, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere just 900 m from the beach. Weekly housekeeping is included, and the property is set in a quiet residential area for complete peace and relaxation.
