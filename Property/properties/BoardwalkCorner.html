About this space

Welcome to Boardwalk Corner, a newly built, self-catering home in the sought-after Boardwalk Estate in Plettenberg Bay’s Robberg Ridge area. Ideally positioned on the corner of the estate, it sits right next to the Robberg Shopping Centre, offering immediate access to shops, cafés, restaurants, and daily essentials, all within walking distance of Sanctuary Beach.

Thoughtfully designed with a boho-tropical coastal style, the home features warm textures, natural elements, and layered colours, creating a relaxed and inviting atmosphere for comfortable coastal living.

The space

The home accommodates up to six guests across three bedrooms and three bathrooms, including two en-suite bedrooms.

Bedroom 1: King XL bed (en-suite)
Bedroom 2: Two 3/4 XL beds (en-suite)
Bedroom 3: Queen XL bed (separate bathroom)

The open-plan layout includes a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, and a comfortable lounge that opens onto a sunny patio with a dining area, outdoor lounge, both a gas braai and a wood braai, and a small private garden with sun loungers.

Guests have access to the communal swimming pool and clubhouse within the estate. The property includes parking bays for two cars.

Inside, the home includes Wi-Fi and a smart TV where guests can sign in to their own streaming accounts. Linen, bath towels, beach towels, toilet paper, body soap, and general cleaning supplies are provided.

Climate control consists of portable fans throughout and a wood-burning fireplace.

Whether you’re looking for a peaceful retreat or a convenient base to explore Plettenberg Bay’s beaches and attractions, Boardwalk Corner offers modern comfort in an excellent location.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Boardwalk Estate is a modern, brand-new development in the peaceful Robberg Ridge area of Plettenberg Bay. Surrounded by lush greenery and coastal charm, it offers a calm, contemporary setting that blends comfort with convenience.

Right next to the estate is a modern shopping center with everything guests might need, from grocery stores and a daytime coffee shop to a pharmacy, barbershop, and beauty services like Gloss & Glow for nails or waxing. (The shopping centre is not open at night.)

The beach is only a short 700 m walk away, and the renowned Robberg Nature Reserve with its scenic hiking trails is just a few minutes’ drive (around 2 km) from the estate.

With its combination of new architecture, natural surroundings, and close proximity to both amenities and outdoor attractions, Boardwalk Estate provides an ideal base for a relaxed and effortless stay in Plettenberg Bay.

Getting around

Guests can easily get around by car, with free parking available on the property. The house is conveniently located near local shops, restaurants, and beaches – all just a short drive or pleasant walk away. Reliable taxi services are also available in Plettenberg Bay for those who prefer not to drive.
