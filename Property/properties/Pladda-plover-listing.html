Pladda Plover

Welcome to Pladda Plover — a freshly renovated coastal retreat sleeping up to 8 guests across 4 bedrooms. Originally built in the 1980s and fully renovated in 2021, this modern home combines comfort and functionality with full solar power, fast WiFi, and a secure motorised gate.

The open-plan living area features a lounge, dining space, and kitchen with dishwasher, microwave, washing machine, and tumble dryer. Enjoy the Bluetooth sound bar, HD TV with DSTV/Netflix access via your own accounts, and a convenient kitchen-counter workspace. Outside, relax on the patio with a built-in fireplace and charcoal braai.

Bedrooms: Main Bedroom (King XL, en-suite), Room 1 (King), Room 2 (Double), Room 3 (Queen). Only the main bedroom is en-suite; the other three bedrooms share a full bathroom with bath, shower, and toilet, plus there is an additional guest loo. Linens and towels are provided, along with hand wash and body wash.


Please Note: Available from 13th to 26th December only, with a minimum stay of 14 nights.
