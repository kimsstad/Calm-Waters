5 Boardwalk

Welcome to 5 Boardwalk — a modern, stylish home in the newly developed Boardwalk Estate, located just a short walk from Sanctuary Beach. Recently finished and beautifully presented, this neat property sleeps up to 4 guests across 3 bedrooms and sits inside a secure estate with a communal pool and clubhouse.

The open-plan living area is furnished in a contemporary style and leads to an outside patio with built-in braai area, seating and sun loungers, plus a small charming garden. The fully equipped kitchen includes a dishwasher, fridge, freezer, stove, oven, washing machine and ample work surfaces. The home is clean, bright and central — right behind a shopping centre and within easy reach of restaurants, shops and retail outlets.

Bedrooms: Two bedrooms are en-suite; the third bedroom is located next to a separate bathroom. The first upstairs bathroom features a bath, shower, toilet and double vanity; the other two bathrooms each have a shower, vanity and toilet. Linens and towels are provided. Please note this property sleeps a maximum of 4 guests.
