The space

Boardwalk Reverie is a stylish, newly built self-catering home in the sought-after Boardwalk Estate in Plettenberg Bay’s Whale Rock area. Thoughtfully designed with modern finishes and soft neutral tones, this upmarket coastal retreat offers a calm and comfortable atmosphere within walking distance of Solar Beach and Sanctuary Beach, and only minutes from the Robberg Nature Reserve viewpoints and hiking trails.

The home accommodates up to six guests across three bedrooms and 2.5 bathrooms, including one en-suite bedroom, one adjacent full bathroom, and a guest lavatory downstairs.

Bedroom 1: king bed, full en-suite bathroom, and private balcony
Bedroom 2: king bed with adjacent full bathroom
Bedroom 3: two large single beds, which can be converted into a king on request

The open-plan layout includes a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, and comfortable lounge that opens onto a sunny private garden. Large glass doors lead to an outdoor entertainment area with a built-in braai, a secondary gas braai option, an eight-seater outdoor dining table, and a cosy patio lounge. The garden offers excellent privacy and seclusion.

Inside, the dining area seats six, with a spacious kitchen and a separate fully equipped scullery featuring premium Bosch and SMEG appliances.

Guests have access to the communal swimming pool and clubhouse within the estate, complete with braai facilities, seating areas, and sun loungers. The property includes a two-car garage plus additional guest parking.

Inside, the home includes uncapped fibre Wi-Fi, a smart TV where guests can sign in to their own streaming accounts, and a DSTV package including major channels and sports. Linen, bath towels, one set of beach/pool towels per guest, toilet paper, body soap, cleaning supplies, and complimentary tea and coffee are provided.

Climate control consists of standing fans throughout and a wood-burning fireplace, with starter firewood provided.

Boardwalk Estate offers 24-hour manned security, CCTV monitoring, electric fencing, and controlled access. The home also includes backup power via generator/inverter.

The estate offers convenient pedestrian access to the beach boardwalk and a nearby shopping centre with a café, supermarket, pharmacy, and other daily essentials.

Beach chairs and umbrellas are provided. No matriculants/ragers.

Whether you’re looking for a peaceful coastal escape or a convenient base to explore Plettenberg Bay and the Garden Route, Boardwalk Reverie offers modern comfort, privacy, and an exceptional location.

Where you'll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Boardwalk Estate is a modern, brand-new development in the peaceful Robberg Ridge area of Plettenberg Bay. Surrounded by lush greenery and coastal charm, it offers a calm, contemporary setting that blends comfort with convenience.

Right next to the estate is a modern shopping center with everything guests might need, from grocery stores and day time coffee shop, to a pharmacy, barbershop, and beauty services like Gloss & Glow for nails or waxing. (The shopping centre is not open at night)

The beach is only a short 700 m walk away, and the renowned Robberg Nature Reserve with its scenic hiking trails is just a few minutes’ drive (2km) from the estate.

With its combination of new architecture, natural surroundings, and close proximity to both amenities and outdoor attractions, Boardwalk Estate provides an ideal base for a relaxed and effortless stay in Plettenberg Bay.

Getting around

Guests can easily get around by car, with free parking available on the property. The house is conveniently located near local shops, restaurants, beaches, and the beach - all just a short drive or pleasant walk away. Reliable taxi services are also available in Plettenberg Bay for those who prefer not to drive.
