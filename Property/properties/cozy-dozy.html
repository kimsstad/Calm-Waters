The space

Cozy Dozy Beach House is a large self-catering home designed for comfortable coastal living. The property features six bedrooms and four bathrooms, making it well suited to families or groups.

Bedroom 1: Queen bed, en-suite bathroom (shower-over-bath), pool and garden views
Bedroom 2: Two single beds, pool-facing (shared bathroom)

Bedroom 4: Queen bed, en-suite bathroom (shower)

The fully equipped kitchen includes a stove, oven, fridge-freezer, dishwasher, microwave, washing machine, tumble dryer, and Nespresso coffee machine. The outdoor area features a generous patio with a built-in braai, ideal for relaxed outdoor meals. Linen, bath towels, swimming towels, and basic toiletries are provided. Parking and gate remotes are included.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Cozy Dozy is located in a peaceful, family-friendly neighbourhood in Plettenberg Bay, offering a relaxed coastal atmosphere close to beaches, local shops, and restaurants.

Robberg 5 Beach is just a five-minute stroll away, making it ideal for early morning walks, sunset dips, and easy beach days. Guests also enjoy nearby access to scenic nature walks, lagoon areas, and some of Plett’s most beautiful viewpoints.

The area is quiet and welcoming, perfect for guests looking to unwind while still being close to the town’s attractions. Please be mindful and considerate of neighbours during your stay.

Getting around

Getting around is easy by car, with free on-site parking and gate remotes provided. Shops, restaurants, and additional beaches are all a short drive away.

Taxi services are also readily available in Plettenberg Bay for guests who prefer not to drive.
