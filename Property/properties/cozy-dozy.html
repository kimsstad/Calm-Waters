Cozy Dozy Beach House

Cozy Dozy is a peaceful, family-friendly getaway just a 5-minute stroll from Robberg 5 Beach. This spacious home sleeps up to 12 guests across 6 comfortable bedrooms (4 queens, 4 singles) and features 3 full bathrooms plus 4 en-suite WCs for no waiting in line. Enjoy a large lounge, dining area, fully equipped kitchen, outdoor braai area, private pool, and fast WiFi—perfect for families or groups wanting to relax, reconnect, and explore nearby beaches, shops, and restaurants.

The open-plan living area includes a lounge, dining room, and kitchen with dishwasher, washing machine, tumble dryer, microwave, and Nespresso machine. Fans in every bedroom and a gas heater in the lounge keep you comfortable year-round. Outside, unwind at the braai area or on the patio with outdoor furniture. An Android TV box provides DSTV and Netflix access with your own account. Linens, bath, hand, and pool towels are provided along with soap and hand wash.

Guests have full access to the entire house, laundry facilities, free parking, and gate remotes. Please respect this quiet neighbourhood and treat the home with care. Bring your own streaming logins for TV services. Rage bookings are possible with parental consent and deposit.


Please Note – No pets unless arranged in advance. Rates vary by season—contact us to book your stay!
