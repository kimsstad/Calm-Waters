SeaSalt Rest

Welcome to SeaSalt Rest — a charming 3-bathroom, 3-bathroom cottage full of classic Plettenberg Bay character. Attached to SeaSalt Escape, this cosy retreat is set in the peaceful Poortjies neighbourhood, just a short stroll from the dog-friendly lagoon, local shops, and restaurants.

The cottage features a relaxed living space with a comfortable lounge and Smart TV, a convenient kitchenette, and two full bathrooms, each with a bath, shower, and toilet. Its simple layout and homely touches capture that old Plett holiday feel — comfortable, unpretentious, and close to everything you need.

In addition to the main cottage, there is a separate outside room with its own en-suite bathroom — ideal for two teenagers seeking their own private space. This extra room makes SeaSalt Rest a flexible option for families or small groups.

Step outside to your private patio with a braai and leafy garden area, perfect for quiet mornings or sundowners after a day at the beach. SeaSalt Rest offers a peaceful and pet-friendly escape in one of Plettenberg Bay’s most central, walkable areas.
