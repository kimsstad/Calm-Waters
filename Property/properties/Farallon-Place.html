About this space

Farallon Place is a beautifully renovated coastal home in Plettenberg Bay, blending natural elegance with comfort in a tranquil setting just 200 metres from the beach. Ideal for families, this bright double-storey retreat is designed for relaxed coastal living and memorable holidays.

Sleeping up to 9 guests, the home features spacious living areas, a fully equipped kitchen, sheltered patio, fire pit, garden, and pool—perfect for both entertaining and unwinding.

The space

Farallon Place is a thoughtfully designed four-bedroom home offering a balance of style, comfort, and functionality. Upstairs, the master suite features a king-size bed, walk-in closet, and a generous en-suite bathroom with bath and shower. This level also includes a dedicated workspace with high-speed Wi-Fi and a private TV room with HD streaming services.

Downstairs are two spacious bedrooms with queen beds and a children’s room with three single bunk beds. One bedroom has an en-suite bathroom with a bath and charming outdoor shower, while the remaining bedrooms share a full bathroom with bath and shower. All bedrooms open via sliding doors to welcome in the ocean breeze.

The open-plan lounge and dining area is filled with natural light and warmed by an indoor fireplace. The fully equipped kitchen includes modern conveniences such as a Nespresso machine, microwave, dishwasher, and ample counter space. A large TV lounge with inverter-backed power ensures uninterrupted comfort.

The sheltered patio leads out to a cozy fire pit, well-kept garden, and sparkling pool, ideal for outdoor relaxation. A second outdoor fireplace and lounge area create a welcoming space for cooler evenings. Laundry facilities, including a washing machine, tumble dryer, and ironing essentials, are provided, along with bath, hand, and pool towels.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Farallon Place is tucked away on peaceful Farallon Street in Plettenberg Bay, offering a tranquil seaside setting just 200 metres from Robberg Beach—ideal for sunrise walks and refreshing ocean dips.

The dramatic cliffs and trails of the Robberg Nature Reserve are only a five-minute drive away, while nearby Whale Rock and Longships Beach provide calm waters and sandy stretches perfect for families and relaxed beach days.

For local flavour and convenience, Plettenberg Bay’s vibrant town centre is just a short drive away, with artisanal cafés, boutique shops, fresh-produce markets, and restaurants. Guests can enjoy the balance of seaside serenity with easy access to town amenities.

Getting around

A car is recommended for exploring Plettenberg Bay and the surrounding Garden Route. Guests are welcome to park in the garage or on the driveway inside the gate.

Robberg Beach is an easy 200 metre walk from the house, and the quiet, safe neighbourhood features paved pathways for short strolls. If transport is needed, guests may enquire with the host about the available chauffeur service.
