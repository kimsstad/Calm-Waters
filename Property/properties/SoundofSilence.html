Sound of Silence

Nestled along the banks of the Keurbooms River, Sound of Silence is a calm and stylish 3-bedroom, 3-bathroom apartment with sweeping garden and water views. Enjoy morning coffee on your private patio, sunset braais, and strolls through the riverfront estate. Perfect for couples, friends or families.

Relax in the open-plan lounge with plush seating, HD Smart TV, and sliding doors to a sunny outdoor dining area. The fully-equipped kitchen includes an air fryer, Nespresso, gas stove, and all essentials. Each bedroom has its own en suite and heater, with two queen beds and two ¾ beds across three rooms.

Communal pool, river-access jetty, walking paths and tennis courts complete the offering. With self check-in, secure parking, and thoughtful touches like stocked basics and swim towels, this is a peaceful base for your Plettenberg Bay adventure.
