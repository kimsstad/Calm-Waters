Ichibi Luxury Lodge

Ichibi Luxury Lodge can be rented in its entirety or as individual suites, each comfortably accommodating 2 guests with a private en-suite bathroom. Named after the Xhosa word for “pond,” the lodge is set among tranquil dams framed by lush gardens and breathtaking mountain views.

We offer four African-inspired suites—Zebra Suite, Hollywood Suite, Ostrich Lux Room, and Elephant Lux Room—each opening onto manicured lawns or overlooking the inviting pool area. With an open-plan living space featuring a cozy lounge, stylish bar, and a modern kitchen, the lodge provides the perfect setting for relaxing or socializing.

Ideal for outdoor enthusiasts, Ichibi is just 10km from the pristine beaches of Plettenberg Bay and close to excellent hiking and cycling routes. A light continental breakfast is included, and dinner can be arranged upon request. The lodge is serviced on weekdays, ensuring you can unwind fully in this luxurious haven.


Please Note: Pricing varies by season, and availability may change. We encourage guests to inquire about current rates and available dates to ensure the best experience during your stay.
