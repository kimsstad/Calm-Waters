Ichibi Luxury Lodge

Nestled among tranquil ponds and lush greenery, Ichibi Luxury Lodge offers a serene retreat for up to 10 guests. Located just 10km from the pristine beaches of Plettenberg Bay, this lodge provides luxury and comfort surrounded by the beauty of nature.

Each of the five spacious, African-inspired bedrooms has its own en-suite bathroom, with four of the bedrooms opening out onto manicured gardens overlooking the peaceful ponds. The open-plan living area features a modern kitchen, a cozy lounge with a fireplace, and a stylish bar area perfect for relaxation.


Please Note Pricing varies by season, and availability may change. We encourage guests to inquire about current rates and available dates to ensure the best experience during your stay.
