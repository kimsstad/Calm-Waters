La Joie de Vivre

La Joie de Vivre is a beautifully designed home nestled in the heart of Plettenberg Bay. This charming house, which accommodates up to 6 guests, features a serene garden and is located just a 2-minute drive or 13-minute walk from one of South Africa's most stunning beaches.

The house offers 3 bedrooms, 2 baths, a cozy living room with a fireplace, and a well-equipped kitchen. The lush garden, blooming with bougainvillea, is a peaceful haven, perfect for relaxation.
