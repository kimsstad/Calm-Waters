The space

Though built in the 1970s, Sea Esta Apartment has been beautifully renovated in October 2025, offering a spacious and comfortable stay in Plettenberg Bay. The 165 m² apartment features three en-suite bedrooms and a welcoming living area designed for relaxation.

The fully equipped kitchen includes a gas stove, dishwasher, Nespresso machine, microwave, and all necessary cookware, crockery, and utensils. The open living space provides comfortable seating and a Smart TV.

Step out onto the large patio, complete with a gas BBQ and loungers—perfect for enjoying sunny days and outdoor meals.

Main bedroom: King-size bed with ample storage and dressing area
Bedroom 2: Single beds with ¾ duvets
Bedroom 3: Single beds with ¾ duvets

All bedrooms have their own en-suite bathrooms, ensuring privacy and comfort for up to six guests.

Kindly note: There is space for one car in the garage, with limited additional parking available within the secure complex. The garage also houses the washing machine.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Sea Esta Apartment is ideally located on the corner of Strand Street and Le Vaillant Street, just two roads back from the heart of Plettenberg Bay. A short 300 m stroll leads up to Market Off Main, a local favorite featuring market-style shops, cozy eateries, and sea views.

From here, it’s an easy walk into town, where boutique stores, cafés, and restaurants line the streets. Main Beach, just 1 km away, offers the quintessential Plettenberg Bay experience.

For more tips and recommendations, check out my Guidebook for a full overview of the best spots in town and the surrounding area.

Getting around

While the town and beach are within walking distance, a car is recommended for sightseeing and excursions further afield. Taxi and shuttle services are readily available in Plettenberg Bay for added convenience.
