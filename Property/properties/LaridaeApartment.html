Laridae Apartment

Welcome to the Laridae Apartment — a beautifully furnished 4-bedroom holiday home in the secure and peaceful Laridae complex. Perfectly located just 1.5 km from Lookout Beach and close to Plettenberg Bay’s Main Street, this serene getaway blends comfort, style, and convenience for a truly relaxing coastal stay.

This fully self-catering apartment features two king bedrooms (one en-suite) and two single bedrooms, comfortably sleeping up to 6 guests. The open-plan living and dining space flows effortlessly to a well-equipped kitchen with a dishwasher, washing machine, and tumble dryer. Unwind with Smart TV, uncapped Wi-Fi, and full access to DStv, Netflix, Showmax, and Disney+.

Outside, enjoy shared access to the complex’s sparkling swimming pool and communal braai facilities. The property is pet-friendly (small pets considered) and includes a complimentary weekly cleaning service, with additional cleaning available on request at your own cost.


Please Note: Available 2 December – 10 January, with a minimum stay of 5 nights. Small pets welcome by arrangement.
