Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Little Lincoln is situated within the Marine View Complex in Plettenberg Bay—affectionately known as “Plett”—along South Africa’s scenic Garden Route. Just a short stroll from Central Beach, guests can enjoy white-sand shores ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and surfing, with the area known for its natural beauty and seasonal dolphin and whale sightings.

Nearby attractions include Robberg Nature Reserve, Monkeyland, and Birds of Eden, as well as hiking trails, ocean safaris, golf courses, and the iconic Bloukrans bungee jump. Within town, guests will find a vibrant dining scene, boutique shops, charming cafés, estate wineries, and scenic lookouts such as Whale Tail.

With its central location and easy access to beaches, restaurants, shops, and Garden Route attractions, Little Lincoln offers an ideal base for both adventure and relaxed coastal escapes.

Getting around

Little Lincoln is within walking distance of Central Beach and several local restaurants, shops, and cafés. Taxis and ride services are readily available, and car hire is recommended for exploring nearby nature reserves and scenic drives. Secure parking is available within the Marine View Complex, with access managed via gate app or remote provided with the keys.
