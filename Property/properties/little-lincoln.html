Little Lincoln

Welcome to Little Lincoln — a cozy, family-friendly holiday apartment in the heart of Plettenberg Bay’s Central Beach area. This two-bedroom, two-bathroom retreat sleeps four and features a full kitchen, private patio with built-in wood/charcoal braai, smart TV, washing machine, and panoramic sea & pool views. Just a short stroll from shops and restaurants, and a 3-minute drive to the beach, it's perfect for relaxed coastal getaways.

Located in the secure Marine View Complex on Marine Drive, Little Lincoln offers: a plush king-sized bed in the main bedroom (with hairdryer), two single beds in the second bedroom, quality linens, extra pillows & blankets, and ample clothing storage. Two bathrooms stocked with fresh bath, hand, and pool towels. The open living area includes a Smart TV (DSTV, Netflix, YouTube), books and games, and a relaxed dining space. The fully equipped kitchen—microwave, stove, oven, fridge/freezer, Bodum coffee press, cookware & tableware—comes with a starter supply of tea, coffee, sugar, milk, and cleaning products on arrival.

Enjoy outdoor meals on the private patio, complete with built-in braai and outdoor furniture. A washing machine, drying rack, iron, and cleaning tools support longer stays. Though there’s no air-conditioning or dryer, the apartment is well-ventilated and quiet. WiFi is available (Network: PlettenbergBay | Password: LittleLincoln), and self check-in is via lockbox and secure gate app. Please note: pets and matric “ragers” are not permitted. Minimum stay 2 nights, maximum 30. House rules follow standard community guidelines for noise, parking, and recycling.
