The space

Captain’s Quarters is a newly renovated ground-floor apartment set within the secure Whale Rock Gardens estate in Plettenberg Bay. Designed for easy self-catering stays, this comfortable one-bedroom retreat is ideal for couples or business travellers wanting a peaceful base close to the beach, Robberg Nature Reserve, and everyday conveniences.

The apartment is within walking distance of Robberg Bay Shopping Centre, where guests will find shops, cafés, restaurants, and daily essentials. The nearest beach is approximately 850m away, while Plett’s main beaches, town centre, nature trails, restaurants, and local attractions are all a short drive from the estate.

The apartment accommodates up to two guests in one bedroom and one bathroom.

  • Bedroom 1: queen bed
  • Bathroom: shower, toilet, and basin

The open-plan living space includes a relaxed lounge with a Smart TV and fibre Wi-Fi, as well as a well-equipped kitchen fitted with modern appliances such as a dishwasher, washing machine, microwave, air fryer, oven, stove, fridge/freezer, and coffee plunger.

From the living area, doors open onto a covered patio with outdoor seating, a Weber braai, and a private garden area. It’s a quiet spot to enjoy a morning coffee, unwind after the beach, or spend a slow afternoon outdoors.

Guests have access to the communal swimming pool within Whale Rock Gardens. The apartment includes parking for one car, additional visitor parking, and controlled security access to the estate.

Additional comforts include linen, bath towels, beach towels, an inverter, rechargeable light, freestanding fan, freestanding heater, and extra blankets for cooler evenings.

Whether you’re planning a quiet coastal break, a work trip, or a convenient base for exploring Plettenberg Bay, Captain’s Quarters offers a comfortable and secure stay in one of Plett’s popular seaside areas.

Disclaimer: Guests stay at their own risk. The owners are not liable for any injury, accident, loss, or damage during the stay.

Where you'll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Whale Rock Gardens is set in Plettenberg Bay’s peaceful Robberg Ridge area, a convenient coastal neighbourhood close to beaches, shops, and natural attractions. The estate offers a secure, relaxed setting with landscaped communal areas, walking trails, and access to a communal swimming pool.

Robberg Bay Shopping Centre is within walking distance, offering shops, cafés, restaurants, grocery options, a pharmacy, and other everyday essentials. Please note that the shopping centre is mainly suited to daytime convenience.

The nearest beach is approximately 850m away, while Robberg Nature Reserve is just a short drive from the estate. Guests can enjoy nearby beaches, scenic hiking routes, birdwatching, seasonal whale watching, and easy access to Plettenberg Bay’s restaurants, town centre, and outdoor attractions.

With its combination of security, convenience, and proximity to both the ocean and Robberg Nature Reserve, Whale Rock Gardens offers a peaceful base for exploring Plettenberg Bay.

Getting around

Guests can easily get around by car, with free parking available on the property. The apartment is conveniently located near local shops, restaurants, beaches, and the beach - all just a short drive or pleasant walk away. Reliable taxi services are also available in Plettenberg Bay for those who prefer not to drive.
