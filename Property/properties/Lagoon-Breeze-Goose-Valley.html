The space

Step into this spacious ground-floor apartment, set within the secure and beautifully maintained Goose Valley Golf Estate. Designed for easy family or group stays, the home offers a relaxed open-plan living area, a comfortable lounge, a full kitchen, and a study area with an additional sleeper couch.

The apartment has three bedrooms and three full bathrooms, each with a bath, shower, toilet, and basin. The master bedroom features a king bed, its own en-suite bathroom, and a private patio area with sun loungers and seating. The second and third bedrooms each have queen beds, with their full bathrooms conveniently located just next door.

The main living area opens onto a large outdoor patio with multiple seating spaces, including a comfortable lounge-style area, a bench seating area, an outdoor dining table, and a Weber braai. It is a lovely space to relax, dine, or enjoy the estate surroundings. From the patio, guests can take in views of the mountains, lush vegetation, and a small glimpse of the lagoon and ocean beyond.

One of the estate's communal swimming pools is conveniently located right next door, and guests have access to two shaded parking bays.

Guest access

The entire apartment is for your personal use, and as a guest you have access to the full resort amenities, including a pool, golf center, championship golf course, playground, trampolines, clubhouse, and restaurant.

Other things to note

Please note that Goose Valley Golf Estate has a secure entrance and exit boom. Guests will need to use the provided key fob each time they enter or leave the estate, so please keep it with you during your stay.

South Africa may occasionally experience electricity restrictions known as load shedding. Should this occur during your stay, a load shedding schedule will be provided where possible.

A few helpful tipping guidelines for services in South Africa:
Waiters/waitresses: 10% of the full bill is the usual guideline for good service.
Car guards: Loose change is appreciated.

Where you'll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Goose Valley Golf Estate is nestled away from the hustle and bustle of the city center, offering a peaceful and tranquil setting in an immaculate and renown golf course. Despite its serene location, the charming town, known for its excellent restaurants and quaint shops, is only 3.9 km away.

In the surrounding area, you can explore various attractions, including vineyards and nature reserves, with bird sanctuaries, cheetah and monkey parks, elephant encounters, a reptile park, and even a puzzle park.

The nearest beaches are just 5km away by car, with several others only a 4 km drive. For hiking enthusiasts, the majestic Robberg Nature Reserve is a must-visit destination.

Getting around

A vehicle is recommended for getting around, though chauffeur services are also available if needed.

No need on the Goose Valley golf estate itself though, the pool is metres away; the lagoon, golf, tennis and squash courts are within walking distance.
