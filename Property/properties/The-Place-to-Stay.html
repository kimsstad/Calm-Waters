About this space

The Place to Stay Apartment is a modern two-bedroom apartment situated in Plettenberg Bay, near Beacon Isle and within walking distance of beaches, cafés, and shops. Centrally located, it offers easy access to the best of Plett’s coastal lifestyle.

Guests are close to the beach, lagoon, restaurants, and boutique stores, with nearby wildlife sanctuaries, nature reserves, and hiking trails offering canoeing, mountain biking, scenic boat trips, and seasonal whale and dolphin watching along the Garden Route.

The space

The apartment accommodates up to four guests and features two bedrooms and two bathrooms. The main bedroom includes a queen-size bed and hairdryer, while the second bedroom is furnished with two single beds.

Both bathrooms are fitted with walk-in showers. Fresh linens, towels, body wash, and extra blankets are provided for guest comfort.

The open-plan living and dining area offers comfortable seating, a TV, ceiling fans, and flows into the fully fitted kitchen. The kitchen includes a microwave, refrigerator, dishwasher, cutlery, crockery, and a French press. Please note there is no stove.

Guests can enjoy the private balcony; however, braaing and smoking are not permitted due to building rules. The apartment is located on the first floor and accessed via stairs. Children should be supervised on the balcony at all times.

This apartment is centrally located in a vibrant urban area, and some city noise may be heard. Exterior windows and doors are fitted with double glazing, and keeping them closed significantly reduces noise. Air-conditioning helps maintain a comfortable indoor temperature.

Guests have exclusive use of the entire two-bedroom apartment, including bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living and dining area, and the private balcony.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

The Place to Stay is ideally located in a vibrant, walkable neighborhood in the heart of Plettenberg Bay, just a short stroll from Central Beach, Beacon Isle Hotel, and the ever-popular River Club area. The apartment is conveniently situated just above a pharmacy, making it easy to grab any essentials you might need during your stay.

Off to the side, you’ll find a SPAR supermarket and the SPAR Liquor Store, perfect for stocking up on groceries or beach picnic supplies. Next door is the well-loved Surf Café, a local hotspot for casual meals, drinks, and live music, with more restaurants tucked below for even more dining options.

This area has a lively, friendly vibe, especially in peak holiday season, with a great mix of cafés, eateries, and shops all within easy walking distance. The nearby roundabout makes beach access simple — you’re about a 12–15 minute walk to the sand, where you can relax, swim, or watch the dolphins and whales that frequent the bay.

Whether you’re after beach days, laid-back dining, or browsing local stores, everything is right on your doorstep — no car needed to soak up the best of Plett’s coastal charm!

Getting around

One of the best things about staying in this apartment is that you really don’t need a car to enjoy the area — most restaurants, shops, cafés, and beaches are within easy walking distance. The neighborhood is very pedestrian-friendly, with the beach about a 12–15 minute walk away, and plenty to see and do along the way.

If you’d like to explore further afield, local taxi services and nearby car hire companies like Avis at the Beacon Island Hotel (just 800 m away) make it easy to plan day trips along the Garden Route.

Parking: Free street parking options are available with 24-hour security patrolling the area, so you can park, unpack, and start exploring on foot.
