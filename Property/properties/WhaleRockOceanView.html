Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Nestled in the secure and upmarket Whale Rock Heights estate in Plettenberg Bay, this home offers peace, privacy, and sweeping ocean and mountain views. The tranquil estate setting allows guests to enjoy nature while remaining close to town amenities.

The nearest beaches are just 3.3 km away, ideal for relaxed seaside days, while the dramatic trails and cliffs of the Robberg Nature Reserve are only 3.5 km from the property.

Everyday conveniences are within easy reach, with Robberg Bay Shopping Centre located just 2.4 km away, offering grocery stores, restaurants, and essential services.

Whale Rock Heights provides a peaceful, secure environment with the perfect balance of privacy, natural beauty, and convenient access to Plettenberg Bay’s beaches, nature reserves, and town centre.

Getting around

A car is recommended for exploring Plettenberg Bay and the surrounding Garden Route. Guests are welcome to park in the garages or on the driveway inside the gate, with space for up to six vehicles.

From Whale Rock Heights, beaches, Robberg Nature Reserve, and local shops are all just a short drive away. The estate itself is quiet and secure, ideal for short strolls, while a car makes getting around simple and convenient.

If you require transport into town, to the nature reserve, or to local cafés, please ask your host about the available chauffeur service.
