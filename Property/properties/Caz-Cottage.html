Caz Garden Cottage

One last stay in this charming gem before it makes way for a luxury development. This quaint cottage in a peaceful garden offers guests a serene escape with spectacular views. Centrally located near town and beach, it provides a perfect blend of convenience and tranquility. Guests are welcome to join the owners on their patio for a sundowner or morning coffee to take in the breathtaking views of the expansive bay and the majestic mountains.

Step into this charming cottage, adorned with antique and vintage furnishings that give it a unique character. The bedroom nook, with its beautifully crafted wardrobe, sits toward the back, while the front features an inviting lounge area complete with a TV, sofa, and a lovely antique half-moon dining table. The kitchen, located off to the side, has all the essentials for a comfortable stay.
