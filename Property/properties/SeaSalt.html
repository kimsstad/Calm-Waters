SeaSalt

Welcome to SeaSalt — a bright, spacious coastal retreat in the peaceful Poortjies area of Plettenberg Bay. This full-property rental combines the main house, a private outside room, and a semi-detached guesthouse, offering a total of 6 bedrooms and 5 bathrooms. Perfect for families or groups of up to 11 guests, it’s just a short stroll from the lagoon and nearby shops.

The main house includes 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The upstairs main bedroom features a king-size bed, private balcony, and en-suite bathroom with shower, toilet, and basin. Two downstairs bedrooms with single beds share a full bathroom with bath, shower, toilet, and basin. The open-plan living area includes a dining space, lounge with Smart TV and WiFi, and a fully equipped kitchen with stove, oven, fridge-freezer, dishwasher, and washing machine. Outside, guests can enjoy a swimming pool, built-in braai, shaded patio, and comfortable seating area.

Adjacent to the main house is a private outside room with two single beds and its own en-suite bathroom — ideal for teenagers or extra guests seeking a little more privacy.

The semi-detached guesthouse features 2 bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom (both with baths and showers), a cozy lounge with Smart TV, a kitchenette with stovetop, fridge, microwave, toaster, and kettle, plus a private garden with a portable braai and outdoor seating.

All linen, bath towels, swimming towels, and basic amenities are provided. SeaSalt is pet-friendly by arrangement, with enclosed gardens and a shared driveway where guests may occasionally meet one another’s pets. The gates between the two garden areas can be opened or kept closed, depending on your preference for privacy or shared space.
