The space

This beautifully designed home balances style, comfort, and functionality, making it ideal for large families or groups of friends. The property includes five bedrooms, three modern bathrooms, two spacious living areas, and two fully equipped kitchens.

The home feels like two houses combined, allowing groups to share the space while still enjoying privacy. Recently renovated with architectural flair, every detail has been thoughtfully considered.

The main section includes three spacious bedrooms overlooking the garden, including a luxurious master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom and alcove bath. Two additional bedrooms share a stylish second bathroom. This section features a large open-plan dining and living area with an indoor fireplace and a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances, flowing seamlessly to the pool and outdoor areas.

The secondary section offers two bedrooms overlooking the front garden and street, each with a double bed and an extra single mattress. This area includes a shared bathroom, a compact kitchenette, and a second lounge with high ceilings opening onto a private courtyard with a Weber gas braai and large TV. It also has its own separate entrance.

Additional highlights include a refreshing plunge pool, pet-friendly accommodation, entertainment extras such as smart TVs, beach games, surfboards, bodyboards, and bikes, as well as an inverter ensuring uninterrupted power during load shedding.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

The little town is small and filled with sweeping vistas encompassing the Tsitsikamma Mountain Range, the breathtaking Robberg Peninsula, and the idyllic surroundings of Plettenberg Bay. A 4 km stretch of beach, extending from Beacon Isle Hotel to Robberg, is conveniently just 500 m from the house.

The downtown valley, a mere 3 km away, hosts supermarkets and delightful beachside and mountainside restaurants. Venture a bit further up the road to the charming town, boasting a variety of fine eateries, art establishments, and diverse shops catering to every taste.

Beyond the renowned Robberg Beach, two additional beaches—Look Out Beach and Main Beach—grace the area just below town. A stunning lagoon also awaits, offering crystal-clear waters and safe swimming opportunities for all ages.

Simply hop onto the N2 Highway to access an array of tourist attractions, including wildlife sanctuaries, scenic walking trails, water sports, and charming vineyards.

Getting around

While the town offers various taxi services, having your own car is the most convenient option. Parking is readily available and easy to find, with well-equipped parking bays and sidewalk parking as an option.
