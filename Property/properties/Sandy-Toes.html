Sandy Toes Home

Sandy Toes Home is a newly renovated, stylish retreat thoughtfully designed with charming nooks and crannies. Located just 500m from the 4km stretch of Robberg Beach, it’s the perfect getaway for large families or groups of friends.

This spacious property features 5 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, 2 fully equipped kitchens, and 2 living areas—ideal for sharing between two families or groups while still enjoying privacy. Unwind by the private plunge pool or savor the garden views from multiple seating areas.
