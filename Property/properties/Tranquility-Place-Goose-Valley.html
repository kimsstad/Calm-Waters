Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Goose Valley Golf Estate is nestled away from the hustle and bustle of the town centre, offering a peaceful and tranquil setting within an immaculate, renowned golf estate. Surrounded by manicured greens and natural beauty, it provides a relaxed environment ideal for rest and recreation.

Despite its serene location, the charming town of Plettenberg Bay is just 3.9 km away, where guests can enjoy excellent restaurants, cafés, and quaint local shops.

The surrounding area offers a wide range of attractions, including vineyards, nature reserves, bird sanctuaries, cheetah and monkey parks, elephant encounters, a reptile park, and even a puzzle park — perfect for families and nature lovers alike.

Several beautiful beaches are within easy reach, with the nearest just 5 km away by car and others approximately 4 km away. For outdoor enthusiasts, the iconic Robberg Nature Reserve is a must-visit destination, offering spectacular hiking trails and coastal scenery.

Getting around

A vehicle is recommended for getting around Plettenberg Bay and exploring the surrounding attractions. Chauffeur and taxi services are also available for guests who prefer not to drive.
