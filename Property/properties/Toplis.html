About this space

Nestled at treetop height with commanding views of the Keurbooms Lagoon, Lookout Beach, and the Tsitsikamma Mountains, this luminous residence is the perfect entertainer’s haven and family retreat. It comfortably accommodates up to 12 guests and blends the tranquility of nature with vibrant urban living, just a 3-minute stroll from the town centre, shops, cafés, and dining options.

Frequent visits from Knysna Loeries add to the sense of being immersed in nature, while the home’s elevated position delivers uninterrupted panoramic views and a peaceful atmosphere throughout.

The space

Distinguished by its contemporary, open-plan layout, the residence harmoniously blends chic aesthetics with practical living. Generous indoor and outdoor areas provide ample space for relaxation, entertaining, and shared moments with family and friends.

The main dwelling features two opulent master suites. The first, located on the right wing of the home, offers expansive proportions, a luxurious en-suite bathroom, and sweeping views of the mountains, lagoon, and ocean. The second master suite, positioned on the opposite side, also includes an en-suite bathroom and opens onto a private inner courtyard.

Two additional bedrooms are located in this wing and share a third well-appointed bathroom. The expansive open-concept living area forms the social heart of the home, ideal for family gatherings and entertaining.

Adjacent to the courtyard, a detached bedroom accommodates two guests in single beds and includes a compact bathroom, making it ideal for teenagers or guests seeking a bit more independence.

On the lower level, a generously proportioned self-contained unit offers complete privacy with its own separate entrance, patio, kitchenette, lounge, and bedroom with a full en-suite bathroom.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

The residence is oriented towards stunning views of the Keurbooms Lagoon and Lookout Beach. Nestled within a verdant green belt, the property enjoys a tranquil, forest-like setting with abundant birdlife and a sense of complete privacy, as neighbouring homes remain out of sight and sound.

Despite the peaceful surroundings, the home is conveniently close to the main street of Plettenberg Bay, offering easy access to leisurely shopping, cafés, restaurants, and scenic strolls through the town.

This unique location allows guests to enjoy the calm of nature while remaining moments away from the vibrant energy of town life and nearby beaches.

For further tips and recommendations, guests are welcome to consult the Guidebook for a detailed overview of the best spots in Plettenberg Bay and the surrounding area.

Getting around

While the town is within walking distance, a car is recommended for most sightseeing and excursions in Plett. Taxi and shuttle services are readily available for added convenience.
