Wildside Escape

Nestled on the cliffs of Plettenberg Bay, Wildside Escape offers breathtaking ocean views and tranquil surroundings. This cabin is designed for those looking for a serene getaway surrounded by nature, with hiking trails and coastal beauty all within a short distance.

The cabin features an open-plan layout with a comfortable living area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a luxurious en-suite bathroom with stunning views. A private deck invites you to relax and enjoy the serene environment.
