Wildside Cabin

Escape to the Wildside Cabin, a peaceful retreat located on the cliffs of Plettenberg Bay. Ideal for couples or solo travelers, this cozy cabin offers a minimalist design and stunning coastal views. Situated on tranquil farmland just 10km from Plettenberg Bay, guests can enjoy nature and scenic hiking trails nearby. Key Features: Open-plan cabin: Includes a bedroom, dining area, and fully equipped kitchen, along with an en-suite bathroom offering panoramic ocean views. Private hiking trail: Access directly from the cabin, perfect for nature lovers. Outdoor relaxation: The spacious deck opens to serene coastal and fynbos landscapes. Communal pool: A refreshing reservoir pool is located just 300 meters away at the main farmhouse. Pebble beach: A 1km hike leads to a secluded pebble beach with rock pools for exploration. Nearby Attractions: Sandy beaches: Pristine white sand beaches and swimming spots are just a 6km drive away. Important Notes: Beach access leads to a pebbled shore, so for swimming, a short drive is required. There is another cabin available for larger groups of up to 4 people. Be mindful of South Africa's electricity restrictions (load shedding), which may affect Wi-Fi connectivity during your stay. This cabin promises a tranquil, nature-filled experience with modern comforts.

  • Location: Plettenberg Bay
  • Bedrooms: 1
  • Bathrooms: 1
  • Amenities: Wi-Fi, Shared Outdoor Pool, Ocean View
