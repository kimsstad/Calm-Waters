The space

Located in the sought-after Tramonto Complex on Sanctuary Beach, this spacious second-floor apartment offers classic coastal character just metres from the sand. With sweeping views of the Robberg Peninsula and glimpses of ocean beyond, it’s an inviting retreat designed for relaxed seaside living.

The large open-plan lounge, kitchen, and dining area open onto a generous balcony, perfect for morning coffee, outdoor meals, or evening sundowners. A dedicated office/study adds flexibility, while a second dining area provides space for shared gatherings. The kitchen includes an inter-leading scullery, ideal for longer stays.

The apartment accommodates up to ten guests across five bedrooms, with two en-suite bathrooms, two shared bathrooms, and an additional guest toilet.

Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Sanctuary Hideaway is located near the end of Beachyhead Drive, one of Plettenberg Bay’s most sought-after coastal stretches. Robberg 5 Beach and Sanctuary Beach are both approximately 150 m away, offering easy access to long, unspoilt sands and peaceful shoreline walks.

Kwikspar Beacon Isle is just 2.2 km from the apartment for convenient grocery shopping, while Surf Café, a local favourite for relaxed meals and live music, is close by. The property is also within easy reach of Market Off Main, a popular local destination known for its sea views, food, bar, coffee, and unique shops.

The Robberg Peninsula hiking trail, famous for its sweeping ocean views and marine life, is around 5.5 km away. Central Beach is 3 km from the property, and the CBD is approximately 3.5 km away, placing restaurants, cafés, boutiques, and essential amenities within easy reach.

The area is peaceful and scenic, making it an ideal base to enjoy Plettenberg Bay’s coastline and outdoor lifestyle.

Getting around

There is a secure parking bay within the complex. Shops, restaurants, and beaches are within walking distance, although a car is recommended for sightseeing and exploring further afield. Taxi and shuttle services are also readily available in Plettenberg Bay.
