Where you’ll be

Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, South Africa

Located at 24 Daedalus Reef Drive in the charming coastal town of Plettenberg Bay, Western Cape, this property offers a serene and scenic getaway just 500 m from pristine beaches.

Enjoy easy access to nature trails, a variety of restaurants and cafés, and nearby attractions such as Robberg Nature Reserve, Monkeyland, and Birds of Eden.

The peaceful neighbourhood provides a tranquil retreat while keeping you close to amenities, making it the perfect destination to relax and soak up Plettenberg Bay’s natural beauty and laid-back coastal vibe.

Getting around

Plettenberg Bay is best explored by car and offers secure, off-street parking for one vehicle. The town centre, beaches, and popular attractions are just a short drive away, with most amenities within a 5–10 minute radius.

While public transport options are limited, the area is bike-friendly for those who enjoy cycling. Walking is also a great option for nearby beaches, restaurants, and nature trails.
