Rondebos Countryside Farmhouse

Nestled in the serene farmland beyond Plettenberg Bay, this charming farmhouse offers a peaceful retreat for families. Combining the calm of the countryside with stunning coastal views, Rondebos is ideal for those seeking to reconnect with nature.

The farmhouse sleeps up to 7 guests, featuring 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms, each with an en-suite. A spacious open-plan kitchen, dining, and living area with modern amenities invites relaxation, while the outdoor patio is perfect for sunset views and BBQs.
